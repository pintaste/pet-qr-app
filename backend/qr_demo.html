📋 How It Works

1. 🎨 Generate QR Codes

Pet owners can generate branded QR codes for their pets. Each QR code includes:

  • Pet's name and branding
  • Secure 4-digit PIN
  • Link to scanning page
  • Professional styling

2. 📱 Print & Attach

The generated QR codes can be:

  • Printed as stickers
  • Made into collar tags
  • Attached to pet carriers
  • Used as keychains

3. 🔍 Scan & View

When someone scans the QR code:

  • Opens scanning page
  • Prompts for PIN entry
  • Shows pet information
  • Provides emergency contacts
